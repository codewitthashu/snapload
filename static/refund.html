Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

1. No Refunds on Used Credits

Credits that have been used for downloads cannot be refunded. Please test the service with your free credits before purchasing.

2. Unused Credits

Unused credits may be refunded within 7 days of purchase, minus a 10% processing fee. Request refunds at refunds@snapload.com.

3. Failed Downloads

If a download fails due to technical issues on our end, we will refund the credit to your account automatically.

4. Account Deletion

If you delete your account, remaining credits are forfeited and cannot be refunded.

5. How to Request a Refund

Email refunds@snapload.com with your account email, payment receipt, and reason for refund. We respond within 3 business days.

6. Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback without requesting a refund first may result in account suspension.

7. Contact

Email: refunds@snapload.com
